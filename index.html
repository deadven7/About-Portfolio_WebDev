ABOUT ME




Hi, I am Amaan Vora.

I am a Master's student from Rutgers University looking to pursue a career in Data Science. I enjoy solving puzzles and problem statements.

My first interactions with a computer began at the age of 2. I would spend the bulk of my days understanding Windows and games like Uno. Fascinated by the world of 1s and 0s, I opted for a Computer Programming course in school, where, at the age of 13, I was introduced to Java. My interest towards computers was cemented through my participation in various competitive computer challenges, where I developed programs, rudimentary gaming software and sporadically indulged in robotics.

Through a Computer Science education for my Bachelor’s, I was inundated with information that pointed to computers being the future. I spent several hours independently researching, and trying projects in multiple fields. Struggling with the grips of the pandemic, it was in 2020 when I turned my sights to Data Science. The field felt tailor-made for my abilities, and as time goes by, this belief grows stronger.

I gained my first work experience developing websites for my father’s pharmaceutical export company. As a sole developer, I had to navigate through the exhaustive pharmaceutical products and packaging solutions offered by the company. These projects inspired me to develop this portfolio website, to highlight my projects, research and certifications.

Over the years, I have increasingly pursued projects on community sites like Kaggle that offer me the opportunity to view a diverse range of problem statements. Each problem has a story of its own, and I find unravelling such stories an enthralling experience.
